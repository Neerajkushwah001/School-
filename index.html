About Us

Roy Vidya Vihar is dedicated to nurturing young minds with holistic education. Located in Bhamruli Katara, we offer a wide range of facilities for both academics and extracurricular activities.

  • Science Lab: Fully stocked with equipment for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology experiments.

Extracurricular Activities

  • Singing: Vocal training sessions to enhance musical talents.
